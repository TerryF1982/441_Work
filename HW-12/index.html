🌟 Orb Collector Game 🌟

HOW TO PLAY:

🎮 Use the Arrow Keys to move the triangle.

🟥 Avoid the large moving rectangles — hitting them costs a life.

🟢 Collect collect all the orbs to advance levels.

💔 You have 3 lives. Lose them all, and it's game over!

🏁 Survive through 5 levels to win the game.
